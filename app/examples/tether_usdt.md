# Tether (USDT) — Example Whitepaper Summary

Tether is a blockchain-based cryptocurrency whose digital coins in circulation are backed by an equivalent amount of traditional fiat currencies, specifically US dollars, held in a designated bank account.

## Token Characteristics

USDT tokens are designed to maintain a 1:1 peg with the US Dollar. Each USDT token is redeemable for one US dollar from Tether's reserves. The token functions primarily as a medium of exchange and store of value in the cryptocurrency ecosystem.

## Reserve Backing

Tether Limited maintains reserves that back every USDT token in circulation. These reserves include traditional currency, cash equivalents, and other assets. The reserves are subject to periodic attestation reports by independent auditing firms.

## Redemption

Token holders can redeem USDT for fiat currency through the Tether platform. The redemption process is available on a daily basis. The redemption policy is clearly outlined in the terms of service.

## Compliance

Tether implements KYC and AML procedures for all users. The company is registered and operates in compliance with applicable regulations. Marketing materials are consistent with the whitepaper disclosures.

## Risk Factors

Investing in USDT carries risks including counterparty risk, regulatory risk, and technology risk. Users should be aware that the value of USDT may fluctuate.

## Issuer

Tether Limited is a company incorporated in the British Virgin Islands. The company is led by a management team with experience in finance and technology.
